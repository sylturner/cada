The Taste of Chamblee

Saturday, October 4, 2014  12 - 7 p.m.


The 7th annual Taste of Chamblee food and music festival will return on Saturday, October 4, 2014 from 12:00pm to 7:00pm in downtown Chamblee (Peachtree Rd. and Broad St.), for a festival of the senses. 

The event will highlight the diversity of Chamblee’s growing community with local fare and entertainment. 



Event entry is FREE. Tickets for food and beverage will be sold for $1 each.
